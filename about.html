About us

Sunny Industries, incorporated in 1981, designs, manufactures, imports and markets Toys & Games. Specializing in manufacturing Stainless Steel Toy Kitchen Sets, Sunny Industries, has made a strong name in the Indian Toy industry. Having added 50+ Skus in recent years the product portfolio has expanded from Role Play Toys to Kaleidoscopes, Jigsaw Puzzles, Board Games, Baby Care and more. Customer-focused approach and the continuous quest for quality, has earned us the trust of 150+ dealers and distributors across India. With introduction of Mall culture and e-commerce, there has been a significant change in the buying habits of consumers. Sunny Industries has gone through a phased process of redefining its overall approach to facilitate growth in the changed market dynamics. Subsequently, Sunny Industries, has managed to add Key Retail Accounts and also, add products to its portfolio that suit their demand. With objective of having Good quality products in the brand mixed with customer centric selling and honest policies, Sunny Industries, is moving towards a promising future.
